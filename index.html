Academics

The curriculum includes a variety of cognitive, emotional, collaborative and linguistic skills. In Language & Literacy children learn about alphabet,stories, rhymes, songs, folk tales. For Math and numeracy they are taught numbers, counting, sorting-ascending/descending order, basic mathematical operations, tables. Additionally, children study about colors, pattern, shapes, calendars, drawing, painting, knowledge of non-gas cooking, practicing health and hygiene habits.
