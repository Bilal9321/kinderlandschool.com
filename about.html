About Us

If we want our children to move mountains, we first have to let them get out of their chairs.
-Nicolette Sowder

We are located in Kondwa camp, Pune

We at Kinderland focus on the child's overall development, as pre-school is the first step for the child to explore a different environment . Here, children are involved in different activities such as running, climbing, and jumping which helps them develop physical coordination and motor skills as well as hand-eye skills are achieved through activities like art and craft.Teachers and other related staff play a crucial role in providing a safe and nurturing environment for young children.

Safety and Security
The staff ensures that children are safe and secure while in their care. They are responsible for maintaining a safe and clean environment, implementing health and safety protocols, and supervising children during playtime and other activities.
Early Childhood Education
Our preschool teachers are trained to provide age-appropriate educational activities that promote children's physical, emotional, and cognitive development. They design and implement curriculum that is tailored to meet the needs of young children and helps them develop social and academic skills.
Emotional Support
Teachers provide emotional support to young children who may be experiencing separation anxiety, homesickness, or other emotional issues. They create a warm and nurturing environment that helps children feel safe and comfortable.
Parental Involvement
The preschool has to work closely with parents to make sure that children receive the support they need both at home and at school. They provide regular feedback on a child's progress and share information about upcoming events, activities, and curriculum.
Early Intervention
Teachers in our preschools are trained to identify and address developmental issues early on. They work closely with parents and other professionals to ensure that children receive the necessary support and services to address any developmental delays or disabilities.

In summary, the teaching and management team in our preschools play an important role in providing a safe and nurturing environment for young children. They help promote children's physical, emotional, and cognitive development, and work closely with parents to ensure that children receive the support they need both at home and at school.
